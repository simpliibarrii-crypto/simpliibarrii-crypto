# Raven AI Ecosystem Banner Map

This file keeps the public banner system organized across GitHub, Hugging Face, and LinkedIn.

## Visual Direction

Use one connected identity across every account:

- Dark graphite / black background
- Crimson and indigo accent colors
- Raven wing geometry
- Clinical AI and biology AI interface motifs
- Clean luxury technology feel
- No platform watermarks
- No Replit branding or tracking language
