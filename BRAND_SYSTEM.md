# Raven Ecosystem Brand System

This document is the source of truth for the visual and verbal identity shared by Barry Clerjuste's original AI projects.

## Brand idea

**Build intelligence with a spine.**

The ecosystem should feel precise, private, evidence-aware, and quietly formidable. It is not a neon cyberpunk collage, a generic SaaS dashboard, or a collection of unrelated experiments.

Crimson and champagne define the brand. Green, amber, and danger pink are semantic colours only.

## Typography

- Display: `Georgia`, `Iowan Old Style`, or an available editorial serif.
- Interface/body: `Avenir Next`, `Inter`, `Segoe UI`, or system sans-serif.
- Metadata/code: `SFMono-Regular`, `Menlo`, `Consolas`, or system monospace.

Display typography should feel editorial. Interface typography should remain clean and practical. Monospace is reserved for traces, evidence, metrics, policies, and runtime state.

## Visual grammar

- Dark obsidian canvas with restrained crimson radial light.
- Fine champagne or ivory borders at low opacity.
- Large editorial headings paired with compact monospace eyebrow labels.
- Rounded panels between 14 and 22 pixels, never bubbly.
- Sparse orbital lines, grids, evidence traces, and node diagrams.
- Motion should be slow and functional. Always respect reduced-motion settings.
- Avoid purple, cyan, rainbow gradients, random badge colours, cartoon mascots, and generic AI brain imagery.

## Maturity language

Use one of these labels consistently:

- Active product
- Active research platform
- Development-preview MVP
- Alpha research software
- Private research prototype
- Attributed integration study

Never replace these labels with unsupported claims such as production-ready, enterprise-grade, clinically validated, or autonomous science.

## Repository header pattern

Every original repository should open with:

1. A product-specific ecosystem banner.
2. Portfolio, demo, research, maturity, and license links using the canonical palette.
3. One sentence explaining what the system proves.
4. An honest maturity notice.
5. Clear quick-start, architecture, safety, and contribution paths.

## Attribution boundary

The `langchain-nvidia` repository is an attributed integration study based on upstream LangChain and NVIDIA work. Ecosystem branding may identify Barry Clerjuste's experiments, examples, and analysis, but must not visually or verbally imply ownership of upstream frameworks.

## Accessibility

- Target WCAG AA contrast for body text and controls.
- Never encode state by colour alone.
- Use visible focus indicators.
- Keep tap targets at least 40 by 40 pixels.
- Support keyboard operation and reduced motion.
